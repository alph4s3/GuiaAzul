Accountancy Academic Organization(AAO)

AAO in Ateneo de Zamboanga University (ADZU) stands for Accountancy Academic Organization. It is a student organization that primarily caters to accounting students at ADZU. The AAO aims to provide a platform for students pursuing accounting and related fields to develop their academic, leadership, and professional skills.

Programs Under AAO:

1.BS Accountancy

2.BS Management Accounting


Education Academic Organization(EAO)

The Education Academic Organization (EAO) at Ateneo de Zamboanga University (ADZU) is a student organization that represents students enrolled in the College of Education. Its primary goal is to support the academic, professional, and personal development of future educators, promoting excellence in the field of education.

Programs Under EAO:

1.Bachelor of Elementary Education

2.Bachelor of Secondary Education

3.Bachelor of Physical Education

4.Bachelor of Early Childhood Education

5.Certificate in Professional Education

History of EAO

The Education Academic Organization is the sole, unified and democratic representative body of its members. All bona fide undergraduate college students of Ateneo de Zamboanga University under the courses of Bachelor of Secondary Education and Bachelor of Elementary Education are automatically members of the EAO. (EAO Constitution and By Laws)


Liberal Arts Academic Organization(LAAO)

The Liberal Arts Academic Organization (LAAO) at Ateneo de Zamboanga University (ADZU) is an academic student organization that represents students enrolled in the College of Liberal Arts. This organization aims to foster intellectual growth, promote academic excellence, and enhance the overall experience of Liberal Arts students.

Programs Under LAAO:

1.BA English Language Studies

2.BA Interdisciplinary Studies

3.BA International Studies

4.BA Communication

5.BA Philosophy

6.BS Psychology


Management Academic Organization(MAO)

The Management Academic Organization (MAO) at Ateneo de Zamboanga University (ADZU) is a student organization that represents students enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Management program. MAO aims to support the academic, professional, and personal development of students pursuing degrees in management and business-related fields.

Programs Under MAO:

1.BS Business Administration

2.BS Legal Management

3.BS Office Administration


Nursing Academic Organization(NAO)

The Nursing Academic Organization (NAO) at Ateneo de Zamboanga University (ADZU) is a student-led organization that represents and supports students enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Nursing program. The primary goal of the NAO is to provide nursing students with opportunities for academic growth, professional development, and community service, while fostering a sense of solidarity and leadership among future nurses.

Programs Under NAO:

1.BS Nursing

History of Nao

The Nursing Academic Organization (NAO) is the official academic organization of the Ateneo de Zamboanga University (AdZU) College of Nursing. It acts as a bridge for communication between the constituents and the university administration while promoting health-related activities and outreach initiatives. The organization is comprised of fixed 12 Executive Committee Members.


Science, Information Technology and Engineering Academic Organization(SITEAO)

SITEAO stands for the Society of Information Technology and Engineering Academic Organization at Ateneo de Zamboanga University (ADZU). It is a student organization that represents and serves the students enrolled in the College of Information Technology and Engineering (CITE), particularly those pursuing degrees in fields related to information technology, computer science, and engineering.

Programs Under SITEAO:

1.BS Biomedical Engineering

2.BS Civil Engineering

3.BS Computer Engineering

4.BS Electronics Engineering

5.BS Computer Science

6.BS Information Technology

7.BS New Media and Computer Animation

8.BS Biology(*Medical Biology*Biological Research*Environmental Biology)

9. BS Mathematics

10. BS Statistics

11. Associate in Electronics Engineering Technology

History of SITEAO

The Constitution of the Science, Information Technology, and Engineering Academic Organization (SITEAO) of the Ateneo de Zamboanga University establishes that all bona fide undergraduate students enrolled in the following programs—BS Biology, BS Computer Science, BS Computer Engineering, BS Electronics and Communications Engineering, BS Biomedical Engineering, BS New Media and Computer Animation, BS Mathematics, BS Statistics, BS Information Technology, BS Civil Engineering, and the Associate in Electronics Engineering Technology—are automatic members of SITEAO. As an academic organization grounded in Ignatian values, we strive to embody Magis, Cura Personalis, Contemplation in Action, Social Involvement, Discernment, Animo, and Excellence. Committed to the ideals of men and women for others, SITEAO dedicates itself to serving its members while fostering meaningful connections with the wider community
